# Sales KPI Power BI Dashboard (2014)
An executive-style Power BI dashboard that tracks actual revenue vs monthly targets, profit trends, and country-level sales contribution. Designed to provide actionable insights for management and sales leadership.

## Skills Demonstrated
- Power BI Desktop – report building, interactive visuals
- DAX – KPI calculations, time intelligence (MoM growth), variance analysis
- Data Modeling – Fact & dimension tables, relationships, target alignment
- Business Intelligence – KPI framework, executive storytelling, data validation

## Business Impact
- Highlights over- and under-performing months against targets
- Reveals seasonal trends and stable profit margins
- Supports data-driven decisions and improves accountability

## Technical Highlights
- Fact & dimension modeling with a clean data structure
- Dynamic KPI measures and Month-over-Month (MoM) growth analysis
- Sales vs Target comparison using validated DAX measures
- Executive-ready interactive visuals and dashboards

## Outcome
Provides management with a `single, interactive view of financial performance`, enabling:
- Faster performance reviews
- Early identification of risk periods
- A scalable foundation for future BI reporting

## Dataset
- financial_dataset.xlsx – Transactional sales data for 2014
- financial_target_dataset.xlsx – Monthly revenue targets reflecting seasonal variation
